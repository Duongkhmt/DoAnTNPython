2. LIGHTGBM LA GI?
------------------

LightGBM la viet tat cua Light Gradient Boosting Machine.

Day la mot thu vien Machine Learning manh, thuong dung cho du lieu dang bang, tuc la du lieu co hang
va cot nhu CSV, Excel, database PostgreSQL.

LightGBM rat phu hop khi du lieu cua du an co dang:

  cot_1 | cot_2 | cot_3 | ... | target

Trong do:

- Cac cot dau vao la feature.
- Cot target la gia tri that ma model can hoc de du doan.

LightGBM duoc dung nhieu trong cac bai toan thuc te vi:

1. Train nhanh.
2. Du doan nhanh.
3. Chay tot voi du lieu dang bang.
4. Xu ly duoc quan he phi tuyen.
5. Co kha nang bat duoc tuong tac giua nhieu feature.
6. Thuong cho ket qua tot ma khong can mang neural network qua phuc tap.
7. Co the xem feature importance de biet cot nao anh huong nhieu.


3. REGRESSOR NGHIA LA GI?
-------------------------

Regressor la model du doan gia tri so.

Cong thuc tong quat:

  y_du_doan = model(x)

Trong do:

- x la bo feature dau vao.
- y_du_doan la gia tri so model du doan.

Vi du:

  Input:
  - feature_1 = 12
  - feature_2 = 0.75
  - feature_3 = "nhom_A"
  - feature_4 = 2025-10-01

  Output:
  - y_du_doan = 83.6

Con so 83.6 khong tu nhien ma co. No la ket qua model tinh ra dua tren nhung quy luat da hoc tu du
lieu train.

5. MODEL DUA VAO DAU DE DU DOAN?
--------------------------------

Model dua vao feature.

Feature la cac cot thong tin dau vao ma model duoc phep nhin thay khi train va khi predict.

Vi du feature co the la:

- Cac cot so: so luong, diem, gia, tan suat, so ngay, so lan xuat hien.
- Cac cot category: loai, nhom, trang thai, khu vuc, nguon du lieu.
- Cac cot thoi gian: ngay, thang, nam, ngay trong tuan, khoang thoi gian.
- Cac cot tong hop: trung binh 7 ngay, trung binh 30 ngay, ty le tang giam, diem lich su.
- Cac cot duoc tao tu text: do dai tieu de, so tu khoa, embedding, diem lien quan.

Model khong dua ra du doan bang cam tinh. No chi tinh dua tren cac feature da duoc dua vao.

Neu mot thong tin khong nam trong feature, model khong the truc tiep dung thong tin do de du doan.

Vi du:

- Neu trong du lieu khong co cot "thoi_gian", model khong the tu biet xu huong theo thoi gian.
- Neu khong co cot "nhom", model khong the hoc su khac nhau giua cac nhom.
- Neu du lieu text khong duoc bien doi thanh feature so, LightGBM khong the hieu truc tiep van ban.


6. TARGET LA GI?
----------------

Target la cot ket qua that trong du lieu train.

Model se hoc cach du doan target dua tren feature.

Vi du:

  feature_1 | feature_2 | feature_3 | target
  ----------|-----------|-----------|--------
  10        | A         | 0.82      | 75.5
  25        | B         | 0.30      | 42.0
  18        | A         | 0.67      | 68.2

Trong bang tren:

- feature_1, feature_2, feature_3 la dau vao.
- target la dap an dung trong qua khu.

Khi train, model duoc xem ca feature va target.
Khi predict du lieu moi, model chi duoc xem feature, roi tu du doan target.

8. DU LIEU TRAIN DUOC CHUAN BI NHU THE NAO?
-------------------------------------------

Truoc khi train, du lieu can duoc xu ly thanh dang model doc duoc.

8.1. Lam sach du lieu

Can kiem tra:

- Dong bi thieu target.
- Cot bi rong qua nhieu.
- Gia tri sai dinh dang.
- So lieu am bat thuong neu khong hop ly.
- Ngay thang sai format.
- Dong trung lap.
- Ky tu thua trong text.
- Gia tri ngoai lai qua lon/qua nho.

8.2. Xu ly gia tri thieu

Gia tri thieu co the xu ly bang:

- Xoa dong neu thieu qua quan trong.
- Dien gia tri trung binh/trung vi.
- Dien gia tri mac dinh.
- Tao them cot danh dau "cot nay bi thieu".

LightGBM co kha nang xu ly missing value tot hon nhieu model khac, nhung khong co nghia la bo qua
buoc kiem tra du lieu. Neu du lieu thieu co y nghia nghiep vu, nen xu ly ro rang.

8.3. Bien doi category

Cot category la cot dang nhom, vi du:

- loai
- trang_thai
- khu_vuc
- source
- category

LightGBM co the lam viec voi categorical feature neu cau hinh dung. Cach khac la ma hoa category
thanh so bang label encoding hoac one-hot encoding.

Quan trong: cach bien doi category khi predict phai giong cach da dung khi train.

8.4. Tao feature moi

Feature engineering la buoc rat quan trong.

Vi du:

- Tu ngay thang tao ra nam, thang, ngay trong tuan.
- Tu lich su tao ra trung binh 7 ngay, 30 ngay.
- Tu text tao ra do dai cau, so tu, so tu khoa.
- Tu nhieu cot tao ra ty le, chenh lech, muc tang giam.

LightGBM manh, nhung model van can feature co y nghia. Feature tot thuong lam model tot hon rat
nhieu.


9. CHIA DU LIEU TRAIN/VALIDATION/TEST
-------------------------------------

Khong nen train va danh gia tren cung mot tap du lieu, vi nhu vay model co the chi "nho bai" thay
vi thuc su du doan tot.

Du lieu nen duoc chia thanh:

1. Train set
   - Dung de model hoc.
   - Thuong chiem 70% den 80%.

2. Validation set
   - Dung de chon tham so, can early stopping, so sanh cac lan train.
   - Thuong chiem 10% den 15%.

3. Test set
   - Dung de danh gia cuoi cung.
   - Model khong duoc hoc truc tiep tren tap nay.
   - Thuong chiem 10% den 15%.

Neu du lieu co tinh thoi gian, nen chia theo thoi gian:

- Du lieu cu hon de train.
- Du lieu moi hon de validation/test.

Ly do: khi chay that, model luon du doan tuong lai dua tren qua khu. Neu chia ngau nhien voi du
lieu thoi gian, ket qua danh gia co the dep gia tao.


10. LIGHTGBM REGRESSOR HOC NHU THE NAO?
---------------------------------------

LightGBM la model thuoc nhom Gradient Boosting Decision Trees.

Can tach thanh 3 y:

1. Decision Tree
2. Boosting
3. Gradient

10.1. Decision Tree la gi?

Decision Tree la cay quyet dinh.

No chia du lieu bang cac cau hoi dang:

- feature_A <= 10 khong?
- feature_B thuoc nhom X khong?
- feature_C lon hon 0.75 khong?

Moi lan chia, cay co gang tach du lieu thanh cac nhom sao cho gia tri target trong moi nhom giong
nhau hon.

Vi du don gian:

  Neu tan_suat <= 5 va diem_lich_su cao -> du doan 70
  Neu tan_suat > 5 va diem_lich_su thap -> du doan 40

Thuc te cay co nhieu nhanh hon va tinh bang cong thuc toi uu.

10.2. Boosting la gi?

Boosting la cach ket hop nhieu model yeu thanh mot model manh.

LightGBM khong chi dung mot cay. No train nhieu cay lien tiep.

Y tuong:

- Cay dau tien du doan mot gia tri ban dau.
- Model xem minh sai o dau.
- Cay tiep theo hoc de sua phan sai do.
- Cac cay sau tiep tuc sua loi cua cac cay truoc.
- Ket qua cuoi cung la tong dong gop cua nhieu cay.

Dang don gian:

  du_doan_cuoi = cay_1 + cay_2 + cay_3 + ... + cay_n

Moi cay khong can hoan hao. Moi cay chi can gop phan sua sai, ca tap hop cay se tao thanh model
manh.

10.3. Gradient la gi?

Gradient o day lien quan den viec giam sai so.

Model co mot ham loss de do muc sai.

Voi regression, loss co the la:

- MSE: binh phuong sai so trung binh.
- MAE: sai so tuyet doi trung binh.
- RMSE: can bac hai cua MSE.

LightGBM dung thong tin ve huong sai so de train cay tiep theo sao cho loss giam dan.

Noi de hieu:

  Model du doan sai chenh lech bao nhieu thi cay tiep theo se hoc cach keo du doan ve gan gia tri
  that hon.


11. LIGHTGBM TINH DU DOAN NHU THE NAO?
--------------------------------------

Sau khi train xong, model gom nhieu cay quyet dinh.

Khi co mot dong du lieu moi:

1. Dong du lieu di qua cay so 1, nhan mot gia tri.
2. Di qua cay so 2, nhan them mot gia tri dieu chinh.
3. Di qua cay so 3, nhan them mot gia tri dieu chinh.
4. Tiep tuc qua tat ca cac cay.
5. Tong cac gia tri lai thanh du doan cuoi cung.

Dang y tuong:

  prediction = base_score
             + learning_rate * output_tree_1
             + learning_rate * output_tree_2
             + ...
             + learning_rate * output_tree_n

Trong do:

- base_score la gia tri ban dau.
- output_tree_i la dong gop cua cay thu i.
- learning_rate la toc do hoc, giup moi cay dong gop vua phai de tranh hoc qua da.

Ket qua cuoi cung la mot con so.


12. NHUNG THAM SO QUAN TRONG CUA LIGHTGBM
-----------------------------------------

Khi train LightGBM Regressor, mot so tham so quan trong gom:

1. n_estimators
   - So luong cay.
   - Qua it cay co the underfit.
   - Qua nhieu cay co the overfit neu khong co early stopping.

2. learning_rate
   - Toc do hoc.
   - Nho hon thi hoc cham hon nhung co the on dinh hon.
   - Lon qua co the hoc nhanh nhung ket qua kem on dinh.

3. max_depth
   - Do sau toi da cua moi cay.
   - Cay sau qua co the hoc qua chi tiet va overfit.

4. num_leaves
   - So la toi da cua cay.
   - Day la tham so rat quan trong trong LightGBM.
   - num_leaves cang lon, model cang phuc tap.

5. min_child_samples
   - So mau toi thieu trong mot la.
   - Tang gia tri nay giup giam overfitting.

6. subsample
   - Ty le dong du lieu duoc lay cho moi vong train.
   - Giup model da dang hon va giam overfitting.

7. colsample_bytree
   - Ty le cot duoc lay khi train moi cay.
   - Giup giam phu thuoc qua muc vao mot so feature.

8. reg_alpha, reg_lambda
   - Regularization.
   - Giup model bot hoc qua ky du lieu train.

9. random_state
   - Giu ket qua train on dinh hon giua cac lan chay.

10. objective
   - Muc tieu train.
   - Voi regression thuong la "regression", "regression_l1", "huber" hoac cac loss phu hop.


13. EARLY STOPPING LA GI?
-------------------------

Early stopping la ky thuat dung train som khi model khong con cai thien tren validation set.

Vi du:

- Model train toi cay thu 500.
- Tu cay 420 den 500, metric validation khong tot hon.
- He thong dung lai va chon vong co ket qua validation tot nhat.

Tac dung:

- Giam overfitting.
- Tiet kiem thoi gian.
- Chon so cay hop ly hon.

Trong LightGBM, early stopping rat nen dung neu co validation set.


14. MODEL DUOC DANH GIA BANG GI?
--------------------------------

Vi day la LightGBM Regressor, cac metric phu hop la metric regression.

14.1. MAE - Mean Absolute Error

MAE la sai so tuyet doi trung binh.

  MAE = trung binh cua |y_that - y_du_doan|

Vi du MAE = 5 nghia la trung binh model sai khoang 5 don vi so voi gia tri that.

MAE de hieu vi cung don vi voi target.

14.2. MSE - Mean Squared Error

MSE la trung binh cua binh phuong sai so.

  MSE = trung binh cua (y_that - y_du_doan)^2

MSE phat nang cac loi sai lon.

14.3. RMSE - Root Mean Squared Error

RMSE la can bac hai cua MSE.

  RMSE = sqrt(MSE)

RMSE cung don vi voi target. Neu RMSE cao hon MAE nhieu, co the model dang co mot so loi sai rat
lon.

14.4. R2 score

R2 cho biet model giai thich duoc bao nhieu phan bien thien cua target.

- R2 gan 1: model giai thich tot.
- R2 gan 0: model khong tot hon nhieu so voi doan trung binh.
- R2 am: model te hon viec doan bang gia tri trung binh.

Can luu y: R2 khong phai luc nao cung du de danh gia. Nen xem cung MAE/RMSE.

14.5. Sai so theo tung nhom

Ngoai metric tong, nen xem sai so theo nhom:

- Sai so theo category.
- Sai so theo khoang gia tri target.
- Sai so theo thoi gian.
- Sai so theo nguon du lieu.

Vi model co the tot trung binh nhung lai sai nang o mot nhom quan trong.


15. NGUOI DUNG NHIN VAO DAU DE BIET MODEL CO ON KHONG?
------------------------------------------------------

Nguoi dung khong nen chi nhin vao mot con so du doan. Can xem cac thong tin sau:

1. MAE/RMSE tren test set
   - Sai so trung binh co nam trong muc chap nhan cua bai toan khong?

2. R2 score
   - Model co giai thich duoc bien dong cua target khong?

3. So sanh train va test
   - Train tot nhung test kem: co the overfitting.
   - Train kem va test kem: co the underfitting.

4. Sai so theo nhom
   - Model co sai nhieu o mot nhom du lieu nao khong?

5. Du lieu test co doc lap khong?
   - Test phai la du lieu model chua hoc.

6. Du lieu co giong thuc te khong?
   - Neu du lieu train khac du lieu chay that, ket qua se kem tin cay.

7. Feature importance co hop ly khong?
   - Cac feature quan trong co dung voi logic nghiep vu khong?

8. Ngay train va phien ban model
   - Model train qua cu co the khong con phu hop.

9. Ket qua du doan co nam trong khoang hop ly khong?
   - Neu model du doan gia tri qua la, can kiem tra input va pipeline.


16. FEATURE IMPORTANCE TRONG LIGHTGBM
-------------------------------------

LightGBM co the xuat feature importance.

Feature importance cho biet cot nao duoc model dung nhieu hoac co dong gop lon trong viec giam sai
so.

Vi du:

  feature_importance:
  - diem_lich_su: 1200
  - tan_suat_30_ngay: 850
  - nhom_du_lieu: 430
  - thang: 210

Y nghia:

- Feature co importance cao thuong anh huong nhieu den du doan.
- Feature co importance thap co the it huu ich, nhung khong nen xoa ngay neu chua test lai.

Can luu y:

Feature importance khong chung minh nhan qua. No chi cho biet model dang dua vao feature do trong
qua trinh du doan.

Nen dung feature importance de:

- Giai thich cho nguoi dung.
- Kiem tra model co dang hoc dung logic khong.
- Phat hien cot bi ro ri dap an.
- Cai thien feature engineering.


17. RO RI DU LIEU LA LOI RAT NGUY HIEM
--------------------------------------

Ro ri du lieu, hay data leakage, la khi feature dau vao chua thong tin ma khi chay that model khong
the biet, hoac chua truc tiep dap an.

Vi du:

- Dung cot duoc tao sau khi ket qua da xay ra.
- Dung cot gan nhu trung voi target.
- Chia train/test sai lam cho cung mot doi tuong xuat hien ca o train va test.
- Chuan hoa/toan bo du lieu truoc khi chia train/test, lam thong tin test ro vao train.

Neu bi data leakage:

- Metric tren test co the rat dep.
- Khi dua vao chay that thi model sai nhieu.

Day la mot trong nhung loi can kiem tra ky nhat khi danh gia model.


18. TRAIN TREN COLAB ROI LUU XUONG NHU THE NAO?
-----------------------------------------------

Sau khi train tren Google Colab, can luu nhung thu sau:

1. File model
   - Chua LightGBM Regressor da train.
   - Co the luu bang joblib, pickle hoac file native cua LightGBM.

2. File scaler/encoder neu co
   - Neu luc train co dung encoder, imputer, scaler, can luu chung.

3. Danh sach feature
   - Rat quan trong.
   - Khi predict, input phai co dung cot, dung thu tu, dung kieu du lieu.

4. Metadata model
   - Ten model.
   - Version.
   - Ngay train.
   - So dong train.
   - Metric MAE/RMSE/R2.
   - Tham so LightGBM.
   - Target du doan.

5. Bao cao ket qua
   - Feature importance.
   - Bieu do y_that vs y_pred neu co.
   - Bang sai so.
   - Nhan xet gioi han model.

Vi du cac file nen co trong du an:

  models/
    lightgbm_regressor.pkl
    preprocessing.pkl
    feature_columns.json
    model_metadata.json
    feature_importance.csv

Khong bat buoc dung dung ten tren, nhung nen co day du nhom thong tin tuong duong.

27. OVERFITTING TRONG LIGHTGBM
------------------------------

Overfitting la khi model hoc qua ky du lieu train va du doan kem tren du lieu moi.

Dau hieu:

- Train MAE rat thap.
- Validation/test MAE cao hon nhieu.
- Train R2 cao nhung test R2 thap.
- Model co nhieu cay, num_leaves lon, max_depth sau.

Cach giam:

- Dung early stopping.
- Giam num_leaves.
- Gioi han max_depth.
- Tang min_child_samples.
- Dung subsample va colsample_bytree.
- Tang regularization reg_alpha/reg_lambda.
- Them du lieu.
- Loai feature gay ro ri dap an.


28. UNDERFITTING TRONG LIGHTGBM
-------------------------------

Underfitting la khi model qua yeu, khong hoc duoc quy luat.

Dau hieu:

- Train MAE cao.
- Test MAE cung cao.
- R2 thap o ca train va test.

Cach giam:

- Tang n_estimators.
- Tang num_leaves.
- Cho cay sau hon neu hop ly.
- Giam regularization neu qua manh.
- Tao feature tot hon.
- Xu ly lai du lieu.


29. KHI NAO CAN RETRAIN MODEL?
------------------------------

Can train lai model khi:

1. Co them du lieu moi da co target that.
2. Du lieu thuc te thay doi.
3. Metric khi chay that xau di.
4. Feature moi duoc bo sung.
5. Pipeline xu ly du lieu thay doi.
6. Target thay doi dinh nghia.
7. Model da cu so voi thuc te.

Quy trinh retrain:

1. Lay du lieu moi.
2. Lam sach va tao feature.
3. Chia train/validation/test.
4. Train LightGBM Regressor moi tren Colab.
5. Danh gia MAE/RMSE/R2.
6. So sanh model moi voi model cu.
7. Neu model moi tot va on dinh hon thi luu thanh version moi.
8. Backend cap nhat sang model moi.


30. MODEL DRIFT LA GI?

Model drift la khi model giam chat luong vi du lieu moi khac voi du lieu luc train.

- Nguon du lieu thay doi.
- Cach crawl du lieu thay doi.
- Hanh vi nguoi dung thay doi.
- Quy luat thuc te thay doi.
- Cac feature co phan phoi khac truoc.

Dau hieu:

- Sai so thuc te tang.
- Prediction lech dan.
- Nhieu input bi ngoai khoang train.
- Chatbot/nguoi dung thuong xuyen nhan xet ket qua khong hop ly.

Cach xu ly:

- Luu prediction va target that de do sai so sau nay.
- Theo doi MAE/RMSE theo thoi gian.
- Theo doi phan phoi feature.
- Retrain khi can.
