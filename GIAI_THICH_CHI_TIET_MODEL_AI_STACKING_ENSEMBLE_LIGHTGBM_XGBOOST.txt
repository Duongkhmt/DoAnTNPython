GIAI THICH CHI TIET MODEL AI: STACKING ENSEMBLE REGRESSOR (LIGHTGBM & XGBOOST)
================================================================================

Tai lieu nay giai thich model AI trong du an theo cach mot nguoi moi nhin vao co
the hieu duoc: model nay de lam gi, no hoc tu dau, dua vao dau de du doan, tinh
toan nhu the nao, train nhu the nao, ket qua doc ra sao, va nhin chi so gi de
biet co nen tin theo hay khong.


1. TOM TAT THAT NGAN GON
------------------------

Model AI trong du an duoc dung de xep hang co phieu Viet Nam theo kha nang
"manh hon thi truong" trong 5 phien giao dich tiep theo.

No khong phai la model du doan chinh xac gia dong cua mot co phieu se tang bao
nhieu. Trong cau truc hien tai, model chu yeu tra ve mot diem ai_score. Diem nay
duoc dung de sap xep cac ma co phieu trong cung mot ngay:

- diem cao: model danh gia co phieu co xac suat/tiem nang vuot thi truong tot hon
- diem thap: model danh gia co phieu yeu hon nhom con lai
- diem trung binh: khong co loi the ro rang

Tu diem ai_score, he thong gan tin hieu:

- TOP_STRONG: nhom diem cao nhat, khoang top 10% trong ngay
- TOP: nhom diem cao tiep theo, khoang top 10%-30%
- NEUTRAL: trung tinh
- WEAK: nhom yeu, khoang bottom 10%-30%
- WEAK_STRONG: nhom yeu nhat, khoang bottom 10%

Muc tieu dung dung: model la cong cu loc va xep hang co phieu, khong phai lenh
mua/ban tuyet doi.

3. MODEL NAY DE LAM GI?
-----------------------

Muc tieu cua model la tra loi cau hoi:

"Trong cung mot ngay giao dich, co phieu nao co kha nang vuot VNINDEX trong 5
phien tiep theo tot hon cac co phieu con lai?"

Tu khoa quan trong la "vuot VNINDEX", khong phai chi "tang gia".

Vi du:

- Co phieu A tang 3%, VNINDEX tang 1% -> A vuot thi truong +2%
- Co phieu B tang 2%, VNINDEX tang 4% -> B kem thi truong -2%
- Co phieu C giam 1%, VNINDEX giam 5% -> C van vuot thi truong +4%

Model tap trung vao alpha, tuc loi nhuan tuong doi so voi thi truong chung.

Trong notebook train_lgbm_ranker_colab.ipynb, target duoc tao theo logic:

alpha_5d = return_5d_stock - return_5d_vnindex

Trong do:

- return_5d_stock: ty suat sinh loi cua co phieu sau 5 phien
- return_5d_vnindex: ty suat sinh loi cua VNINDEX sau 5 phien
- alpha_5d: muc co phieu manh/yếu hon VNINDEX trong 5 phien

Sau do moi ngay se xep hang alpha_5d cua tat ca co phieu:

alpha_rank_pct = thu hang phan tram cua alpha_5d trong ngay do

Day la target de model hoc.


4. TAI SAO KHONG DU DOAN GIA DONG?
----------------------------------

Du doan gia dong tuyet doi rat kho vi gia chiu anh huong boi thi truong chung,
tin tuc, dong tien, bien dong nganh, thanh khoan, tin doanh nghiep, tam ly nha
dau tu.

Du an nay chon bai toan ranking/cross-sectional ranking hop ly hon:

- khong can noi chinh xac gia FPT se la bao nhieu sau 5 ngay
- chi can xep FPT cao hon hay thap hon cac ma khac trong cung ngay
- neu model sap xep tot, nguoi dung co the loc ra nhom co xac suat vuot thi
  truong cao hon

Trong chung khoan, viec "chon dung nhom manh hon thi truong" thuong thuc te hon
viec "doan dung gia chinh xac".


5. DU LIEU MODEL HOC TU DAU?
---------------------------

Du lieu train lay tu file all_stocks_train.csv va cac bang database:

- quote_history: gia open, high, low, close, volume theo ngay
- technical_indicators: cac chi bao ky thuat tinh tu gia va khoi luong
- listing/company: san giao dich, nganh, thong tin co phieu
- VNINDEX trong technical_indicators: dai dien thi truong chung

Pipeline du lieu co the hieu nhu sau:

1. Lay lich su gia co phieu: open, high, low, close, volume.
2. compute_indicators.py tinh cac chi bao ky thuat.
3. Tao target return_5d: loi nhuan sau 5 phien.
4. So sanh return_5d cua tung co phieu voi return_5d cua VNINDEX.
5. Tao alpha_5d.
6. Xep hang alpha_5d trong tung ngay thanh alpha_rank_pct.
7. Train model de hoc quan he giua feature hom nay va alpha_rank_pct sau 5 phien.

7. CAC FEATURE DAU VAO CO Y NGHIA GI?
-------------------------------------

Model production hien tai dung 10 feature.

7.1. price_vs_sma5

Do gia hien tai dang cao/thap hon duong trung binh 5 phien bao nhieu.

Y nghia:

- gia tren SMA5: xu huong rat ngan han dang khoe
- gia duoi SMA5: ap luc yeu ngan han

Cong thuc gan dung:

price_vs_sma5 = (close - SMA5) / SMA5

7.2. price_vs_sma20

Do gia hien tai so voi trung binh 20 phien.

Y nghia:

- SMA20 dai dien xu huong ngan-trung han
- gia nam tren SMA20 co the cho thay co phieu dang trong pha khoe
- qua cao so voi SMA20 co the cung la dau hieu qua nong

Cong thuc:

price_vs_sma20 = (close - SMA20) / SMA20

7.3. rel_momentum_5

Do suc manh 5 phien cua co phieu so voi VNINDEX.

Cong thuc trong daily_predict.py:

rel_momentum_5 = price_momentum_5 - vnindex_momentum_5

Neu rel_momentum_5 > 0, co phieu dang manh hon VNINDEX trong 5 phien gan day.

7.4. rel_momentum_20

Tuong tu rel_momentum_5 nhung trong khung 20 phien.

Cong thuc:

rel_momentum_20 = price_momentum_20 - vnindex_momentum_20

Day la feature rat quan trong vi no do suc manh tuong doi dai hon, giup model
nhan ra co phieu dang outperform thi truong hay khong.

7.5. rsi_14

RSI 14 phien do do manh/yếu dong luong gia.

Y nghia thong dung:

- RSI cao: luc tang manh, nhung qua cao co the qua mua
- RSI thap: luc yeu, nhung qua thap co the qua ban

Model khong doc RSI theo quy tac co dinh kieu "RSI > 70 la ban". No hoc tu du
lieu xem trong boi canh nao RSI cao/thap dan den alpha tot hay xau.

7.6. rsi_vs_vnindex

Do RSI cua co phieu lech voi RSI cua VNINDEX.

Cong thuc:

rsi_vs_vnindex = rsi_14_stock - vnindex_rsi

Neu rsi_vs_vnindex > 0, co phieu co dong luong manh hon thi truong chung.

7.7. volume_ratio

Do khoi luong hien tai so voi trung binh 20 phien.

Cong thuc:

volume_ratio = volume / volume_sma_20

Y nghia:

- volume_ratio > 1: khoi luong lon hon trung binh
- volume_ratio tang kem gia tang co the la dong tien vao
- volume_ratio tang kem gia giam co the la ap luc ban

Model se hoc tu ket hop volume_ratio voi cac feature khac.

7.8. bb_width_norm

Do do rong Bollinger Band da chuan hoa.

Y nghia:

- band hep: bien dong thap, co the dang tich luy
- band rong: bien dong cao, co the co pha bung no hoac rui ro lon

7.9. cmf_20

CMF la Chaikin Money Flow 20 phien, do dong tien dua tren vi tri dong cua trong
range gia va khoi luong.

Y nghia:

- CMF duong: dong tien co xu huong tich cuc
- CMF am: dong tien co xu huong tieu cuc

7.10. atr_pct

ATR do bien dong gia. atr_pct la ATR chia cho gia dong cua.

Y nghia:

- atr_pct cao: co phieu bien dong manh, rui ro cao hon
- atr_pct thap: bien dong nho hon

Model dung feature nay de hieu muc do rui ro/bien dong cua co phieu.

9. LIGHTGBM HOAT DONG NHU THE NAO?
----------------------------------

LightGBM la mo hinh Gradient Boosting Decision Tree.

Giai thich don gian:

- Decision Tree la cay quyet dinh, chia du lieu theo cac dieu kien.
- Vi du: neu rel_momentum_20 > 0.05 thi di nhanh sang nhanh A, neu khong thi
  sang nhanh B.
- Mot cay don le thuong yeu va de sai.
- Gradient Boosting tao nhieu cay lien tiep. Moi cay moi hoc sua loi cua cac cay
  truoc.

Quy trinh hoc gan dung:

1. Model ban dau du doan mot gia tri trung binh.
2. Tinh sai so giua du doan va target alpha_rank_pct that.
3. Train cay tiep theo de giam sai so.
4. Lap lai nhieu lan.
5. Ket qua cuoi la tong hop cua nhieu cay.

Trong file model hien tai:

- objective=regression
- so feature: 10
- max_feature_idx=9
- cac tree duoc luu truc tiep trong lgbm_alpha_5d_ranker.txt

Trong notebook train, tham so chinh:

- objective='regression'
- metric='rmse'
- learning_rate=0.03
- n_estimators=100
- max_depth=3
- num_leaves=8
- min_child_samples=100
- subsample=0.7
- colsample_bytree=0.5
- random_state=42

Y nghia:

- max_depth=3, num_leaves=8: cay nho, giam overfit
- min_child_samples=100: moi nut la phai co du nhieu mau, tranh hoc chi tiet qua
  nho
- subsample=0.7: moi cay chi dung mot phan du lieu, tang kha nang tong quat
- colsample_bytree=0.5: moi cay chi dung mot phan feature, giam phu thuoc vao mot
  feature
- learning_rate=0.03: hoc cham hon, on dinh hon
- n_estimators=100: tao 100 cay


10. XGBOOST HOAT DONG NHU THE NAO?
----------------------------------

XGBoost cung la Gradient Boosting Decision Tree, tuong tu LightGBM, nhung cach
xay cay, toi uu va regularization co khac.

Ve mat y tuong:

- LightGBM va XGBoost deu hoc bang nhieu cay quyet dinh
- moi cay sau sua loi cua cac cay truoc
- ca hai rat manh voi du lieu bang/tabular nhu chi bao ky thuat chung khoan

Ly do dung ca hai trong ensemble:

- LightGBM co the bat mot so mau hinh tot
- XGBoost co the bat mau hinh khac
- neu loi sai cua hai model khong giong nhau, ket hop chung co the on dinh hon


11. STACKING ENSEMBLE REGRESSOR LA GI?
--------------------------------------

Stacking la cach ket hop nhieu model theo 2 tang.

Tang 1: base models

- LightGBM Regressor
- XGBoost Regressor

Moi model nhan cung feature dau vao va dua ra du doan rieng:

- lgb_pred
- xgb_pred

Tang 2: meta model

- Ridge Regression

Meta model khong nhan truc tiep 10 feature goc. No nhan du doan cua hai model
tang 1:

meta_X = [lgb_pred, xgb_pred]

Sau do Ridge hoc cach tron hai du doan nay de gan voi target that nhat.

Cong thuc gan dung:

final_score = intercept + w1 * lgb_pred + w2 * xgb_pred

Trong do:

- w1 la trong so cua LightGBM
- w2 la trong so cua XGBoost
- intercept la hang so dieu chinh

Neu Ridge hoc ra:

- w1 lon hon w2: LightGBM dang co dong gop lon hon
- w2 lon hon w1: XGBoost dang co dong gop lon hon
- mot trong so am: meta model dang dung model do theo huong dieu chinh nguoc

Trong train_ensemble_ranker.py, sau khi train, code in ra:

Meta-learner weights: LGBM=..., XGB=...

Day la cach biet meta model dang tin model nao hon.


12. TRAIN STACKING ENSEMBLE TRONG train_ensemble_ranker.py NHU THE NAO?
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Ham train_ensemble(df, features, target) lam cac buoc:

1. Sap xep du lieu theo trading_date.

   df = df.sort_values('trading_date')

   Vi chung khoan la du lieu thoi gian, khong duoc tron lung tung. Neu tron lung
   tung, model co the nhin thay thong tin tuong lai va ket qua danh gia bi ao.

2. Loai cac dong thieu feature hoac target.

   dropna(subset=features + [target])

3. Chia du lieu theo thoi gian 80/20.

   - 80% dau: train_df
   - 20% sau: val_df

   Day la cach chia theo qua khu -> tuong lai. Khong lay du lieu tuong lai de
   train cho qua khu.

4. Train LightGBM tren train_df.

   lgb_model = lgb.LGBMRegressor(n_estimators=100, random_state=42)

5. Train XGBoost tren train_df.

   xgb_model = xgb.XGBRegressor(n_estimators=100, random_state=42)

6. Cho hai model du doan tren val_df.

   - lgb_val_preds
   - xgb_val_preds

7. Tao input cho meta model.

   meta_X = column_stack(lgb_val_preds, xgb_val_preds)

8. Train Ridge Regression tren meta_X va y_val.

9. Luu ca 3 model vao ensemble_model.pkl.

   - lgbm
   - xgb
   - meta

Can luu y: cach train nay don gian. Neu muon stacking chat che hon, nen tao
out-of-fold prediction bang TimeSeriesSplit/Walk-forward thay vi chi dung mot
tap validation 20% cuoi.

14.7. Gan tin hieu

assign_ranker_signals() sap xep theo ai_score trong cung ngay:

rank_pct nho nhat. Vi vay top 10% co rank_pct <= 0.10.

- vnindex_momentum_20 < -0.02
- hoac vnindex_rsi < 45

thi cac tin hieu TOP/TOP_STRONG bi ha xuong NEUTRAL.

Ngay ca khi co phieu co diem tot, neu thi truong chung dang xau, he thong giam

16. NHIN GI DE BIET MODEL CO OKE KHONG?
---------------------------------------

Khong nen danh gia model bang cam giac hoac mot vai ma du doan dung/sai. Can nhin
theo tap hop va theo thoi gian.

16.1. Rank IC

Rank IC la tuong quan Spearman giua diem model va alpha_5d that.

No tra loi cau hoi:

"Co phieu model cho diem cao co that su co alpha cao hon co phieu model cho diem
thap khong?"

Neu Rank IC:

- > 0: model xep hang co xu huong dung
- gan 0: model gan nhu khong co loi the
- < 0: model xep hang nguoc, nguy hiem

Voi chung khoan, Rank IC khong can qua lon moi co y nghia. Dieu quan trong la no
duong va on dinh qua nhieu ngay/fold.

16.2. Alpha trung binh cua nhom TOP

Kiem tra nhom TOP/TOP_STRONG sau 5 phien:

avg_alpha = trung binh alpha_5d cua nhom duoc chon

Neu avg_alpha > 0, nhom duoc model chon trung binh vuot VNINDEX.

16.3. Alpha win rate

alpha_win_rate = ty le ma co alpha_5d > 0

Neu nhom TOP co alpha_win_rate cao hon 50% va cao hon universe trung binh, model
co dau hieu huu ich.

16.4. Return trung binh

Can xem ca:

- avg_stock_return: co phieu co tang tien tuyet doi khong
- avg_alpha: co vuot thi truong khong

Co ngay thi truong giam, TOP co the van am tien nhung alpha duong. Nghia la no
giam it hon thi truong. Tuy vao chien luoc, dieu nay co the van la thanh cong ve
relative performance.

16.5. So sanh voi universe

Model chi co y nghia neu nhom TOP tot hon mat bang chung.

Can so:

- TOP_STRONG avg_alpha voi universe avg_alpha
- TOP avg_alpha voi universe avg_alpha
- TOP stock_win_rate voi universe stock_win_rate

16.6. On dinh qua thoi gian

Mot model oke khong chi thang mot thang. Can xem:

- nhieu fold walk-forward
- nhieu giai doan thi truong: uptrend, sideway, downtrend
- cac ngay co thanh khoan khac nhau

16.7. Drawdown va rui ro

Neu bien thanh chien luoc mua ban, can xem:

- loi nhuan trung binh
- drawdown
- turnover
- phi giao dich
- slippage
- ty le tin hieu qua nhieu ngay

Model xep hang tot chua chac chien luoc giao dich da tot neu phi cao hoac mua ban
qua nhieu.

19. GIOI HAN VA RUI RO
----------------------

19.1. Model khong biet tin tuc neu tin tuc khong nam trong du lieu

Neu co tin bat ngo ve doanh nghiep, phap ly, chia co tuc, ket qua kinh doanh,

19.2. Model hoc tu qua khu

Neu cau truc thi truong thay doi, quan he cu co the kem hieu qua.

19.3. ai_score khong phai xac suat chac chan tang

ai_score la diem xep hang du kien. Khong doc thanh:

"score 0.8 nghia la chac chan tang 80%".

Doc dung:

"score cao nghia la model xep ma nay cao trong universe ngay hom nay".

19.4. TOP khong dong nghia mua ngay

- thi truong chung
- thanh khoan
- vi tri gia

19.5. Stacking ensemble hien chua noi vao daily_predict.py

ensemble_model.pkl va tinh:

1. lgb_pred = lgbm.predict(X)
2. xgb_pred = xgb.predict(X)
3. final_score = meta.predict([[lgb_pred, xgb_pred]])
4. dung final_score de xep hang va gan ai_signal

Neu khong sua, he thong van dung LightGBM .txt.

19.6. backfill_predictions.py co dau hieu chua dong bo voi daily_predict.py

File backfill_predictions.py import load_features tu daily_predict.py, nhung

20. NEU MUON GIAI THICH "NO TINH TOAN NHU NAO" BANG MOT VI DU
--------------------------------------------------------------

Gia su ngay 2026-06-02, he thong co 300 ma du dieu kien.

Voi moi ma, he thong lay 10 feature.

Vi du ma ABC:

- price_vs_sma5 = 0.03: gia cao hon SMA5 3%
- price_vs_sma20 = 0.08: gia cao hon SMA20 8%
- rel_momentum_5 = 0.04: 5 phien gan day manh hon VNINDEX 4%
- rel_momentum_20 = 0.10: 20 phien gan day manh hon VNINDEX 10%
- rsi_14 = 62
- rsi_vs_vnindex = 8
- volume_ratio = 1.8
- bb_width_norm = 0.02
- cmf_20 = 0.15
- atr_pct = 0.035

LightGBM dua bo feature nay qua nhieu cay quyet dinh.

Mot cay co the hoc logic dang:

- neu rel_momentum_20 cao
- va cmf_20 duong
- va volume_ratio cao
- va rsi khong qua cuc doan
- thi cong diem

Mot cay khac co the hoc:

- neu atr_pct qua cao
- va gia qua xa SMA20
- thi tru diem vi rui ro rung lac

Sau 100 cay, model tong hop thanh ai_score.

Sau khi co ai_score cho 300 ma, he thong sap xep:

- 30 ma diem cao nhat -> TOP_STRONG
- 60 ma tiep theo -> TOP
- nhom giua -> NEUTRAL
- 60 ma gan cuoi -> WEAK
- 30 ma cuoi -> WEAK_STRONG

Neu VNINDEX dang bearish, TOP/TOP_STRONG bi ha xuong NEUTRAL.


21. VI SAO CAN CHIA TRAIN/VALIDATION THEO THOI GIAN?
-----------------------------------------------------

Trong du lieu chung khoan, neu tron ngau nhien toan bo dong roi chia train/test,
co the xay ra ro ri tuong lai.

Vi du:

- dong nam 2025 vao train
- dong nam 2023 vao test

Khi do model da hoc mot phan cau truc tuong lai roi quay lai du doan qua khu.
Ket qua test se dep gia tao.

Chia dung la:

- train bang du lieu cu
- validation/test bang du lieu sau do

Notebook da dung walk-forward:

- fold 1: train mot doan qua khu, test doan sau
- fold 2: lui/cuon sang doan khac
- lap lai 5 fold

Day la cach danh gia gan thuc chien hon.


22. STACKING ENSEMBLE CO THE TOT HON LIGHTGBM DON LE KHI NAO?
-------------------------------------------------------------

Stacking co the tot hon khi:

- LightGBM va XGBoost co loi sai khac nhau
- ca hai model deu co chat luong kha
- meta model duoc train bang validation/out-of-fold prediction sach
- tap validation du lon va dai dien cho tuong lai

Stacking co the te hon khi:

- validation qua nho
- base model qua giong nhau
- meta model hoc qua khop voi validation
- du lieu thi truong thay doi

Vi vay, khong phai cu ensemble la tot hon. Phai so sanh bang:

- Rank IC
- avg alpha cua TOP
- alpha win rate
- do on dinh qua fold
- ket qua out-of-sample


23. NGUOI DUNG NEN THEO MODEL NHU THE NAO?
------------------------------------------

Nen dung model nhu bo loc uu tien:

1. Xem market overview truoc.
   - VNINDEX co dang bearish khong?
   - market breadth co nghieng ve tang hay giam khong?

2. Loc TOP_STRONG/TOP.
   - Uu tien ma co ai_score cao.
   - Xem nganh nao dang co nhieu ma diem cao.

3. Vao detail tung ma.
   - Xem gia co qua xa SMA khong.
   - RSI co qua nong khong.
   - Volume co ung ho khong.
   - Dong tien co tich cuc khong.

4. Kiem tra lich su ai_score.
   - Diem dang tang dan hay chi moi nhay mot ngay?
   - Tin hieu co on dinh hay dao chieu lien tuc?

5. Khong vao lenh chi vi mot chu TOP_STRONG.
   - Can diem mua.
   - Can stop loss.
   - Can quan tri ty trong.


24. CHECKLIST DANH GIA MODEL CO DANG TIN HAY KHONG
---------------------------------------------------

Nen tin hon khi:

- Rank IC duong va on dinh qua nhieu fold.
- TOP_STRONG co avg_alpha > universe.
- TOP_STRONG co alpha_win_rate cao hon universe.
- Ket qua test sau TEST_CUTOFF van on.
- Tin hieu khong qua nhieu, khong dao chieu vo ly moi ngay.
- Model hoat dong duoc tren nhieu giai doan thi truong.
- Feature khong bi thieu nhieu.
- Du lieu dau vao sach va cap nhat.

Can than khi:

- Rank IC gan 0 hoac am.
- TOP_STRONG thua universe trong nhieu ngay lien tiep.
- Model chi tot trong backtest nhung kem tren du lieu moi.
- So ma skipped do thieu feature qua nhieu.
- Thi truong bearish nhung model van cho qua nhieu TOP.
- Co thay doi lon ve du lieu, cach tinh indicator, hoac universe.
