TAI LIEU FLOW HOAT DONG DU AN - TRANG THAI HIEN TAI
==================================================

CAP NHAT MOI:
- He thong da duoc toi uu de giam tai. AI Service da duoc go bo khoi docker-compose va luong chay tu dong vi qua nang.
- DAG hien tai trong Airflow chi con 2 buoc: sync du lieu -> compute indicators.
- Phan tich va du bao AI se duoc thuc hien thu cong bang cach chay script rieng (tren may host) khi can thiet.

1. MUC TIEU CHUNG
-----------------
Du an nay la mot he thong thu thap du lieu chung khoan Viet Nam, luu tru vao
TimescaleDB/PostgreSQL, tinh chi bao ky thuat va xuat du lieu cho mo hinh LSTM.
(Phan AI service hien da duoc tach ra chay thu cong de tiet kiem tai nguyen)

Trang thai hien tai cua du an co 3 lop chinh:
- Lop ha tang va dieu phoi: Docker Compose + Airflow + Redis + PostgreSQL + MongoDB
- Lop ETL va luu tru: script sync du lieu vao TimescaleDB va raw payload vao MongoDB
- Lop phan tich (chay tu dong hoac thu cong): tinh technical indicators, export data cho LSTM.


2. CAC THANH PHAN CHINH TRONG REPO
----------------------------------
- docker-compose.yaml
  Dung cac service ha tang.

- vnstock_sync_dag.py
  DAG Airflow de chay dong bo du lieu hang ngay va goi AI service.

- test.py
  Script sync chinh. Day la trung tam ETL hien tai.

- timescale_utils.py
  Quan ly schema va ket noi TimescaleDB/PostgreSQL cho du lieu stock.

- mongo_utils.py
  Quan ly MongoDB de luu raw payload va crawl logs.

- compute_indicators.py
  Tinh technical indicators tu quote_history va ghi vao technical_indicators.

- export_data_lstm.py
  Xuat du lieu 1 ma co phieu de train/predict LSTM.

- export_all_lstm.py
  Xuat du lieu toan thi truong de train model tong.

- ai_service.py
  FastAPI service dung mo hinh .h5 de tao du bao va ghi vao ml_predictions.


3. HA TANG KHOI DONG BAN DAU
----------------------------
Khi khoi dong bang docker-compose, du an hien tai co cac service sau:

- postgres
  Database metadata cho Airflow.

- redis
  Broker/result backend cho CeleryExecutor cua Airflow.

- mongodb
  Kho NoSQL moi duoc them vao de luu raw payload va crawl logs.

- airflow-webserver
- airflow-scheduler
- airflow-worker
- airflow-triggerer
- airflow-init
- flower
  Cum Airflow de dieu phoi pipeline.

Luu y:
- PostgreSQL trong docker-compose la DB cho Airflow.
- Du lieu chung khoan cua du an duoc code ket noi qua DatabaseManager trong
  timescale_utils.py, mac dinh la DB `vnstock_ts` o cong 5433.
- MongoDB mac dinh dung DB `vnstock_raw`.


4. FLOW TONG THE TU DAU DEN CUOI
--------------------------------
Flow hien tai nen duoc hieu theo 2 nhanh:

A. Nhanh van hanh chinh (Tu dong hoa hoan toan)
- Airflow chay DAG hang ngay
- DAG goi script sync du lieu (test.py)
- DAG goi script tinh toan chi bao (compute_indicators.py)

B. Nhanh xu ly phu tro AI/ML (Thu cong)
- Xuat du lieu huan luyen (export_data_lstm.py)
- Train model ben ngoai hoac dung model co san (.h5)
- Chay thu cong AI service hoac script predict de sinh du bao

Quan trong:
- Bang technical_indicators hien da duoc tich hop vao DAG chay tu dong moi ngay sau khi sync du lieu de dam bao data phuc vu cho chay AI tay luon la moi nhat.


5. FLOW CHI TIET - KHOI DONG AIRFLOW
------------------------------------
Buoc 1:
- Chay `docker compose up -d`
- Airflow init tao metadata DB, user admin, khoi dong scheduler/webserver/worker

Buoc 2:
- Airflow nap file DAG tu repo
- DAG hien tai la `vnstock_daily_sync`

Buoc 3:
- Scheduler den lich se trigger DAG
- schedule_interval hien tai trong DAG la `0 11 * * 1-5`
  Nghia la chay 11:00 UTC thu 2 den thu 6
  Tuong duong 18:00 gio Viet Nam neu server tinh theo UTC


6. FLOW CHI TIET - DAG AIRFLOW
------------------------------
Trong vnstock_sync_dag.py co 2 task chinh:

Task 1: run_daily_sync
- Lenh: `python /opt/airflow/dags/test.py --daily`
- Muc tieu: dong bo du lieu thi truong moi nhat

Task 2: compute_daily_indicators
- Lenh: `python /opt/airflow/dags/compute_indicators.py --daily`
- Muc tieu: tinh toan chi bao ky thuat moi nhat tu du lieu vua dong bo

Thu tu:
- run_daily_sync >> compute_daily_indicators


7. FLOW CHI TIET - TEST.PY (MASTER SYNC)
----------------------------------------
test.py la script ETL trung tam hien tai.

7.1. Khoi tao
- Them duong dan site-packages cua venv khi project duoc mount vao Docker
- Tao DatabaseManager cho `vnstock_ts`
- Goi `create_all_tables()` de dam bao schema ton tai
- Tao MongoManager
- Tao `RUN_ID` duy nhat cho moi lan sync

7.2. Xac dinh che do chay
- Neu co `--daily`:
  START_DATE = now - 3 ngay
  Muc tieu la quet bu de tranh hut du lieu cuoi tuan/ngay nghi

- Neu khong co `--daily`:
  START_DATE = 2024-01-01
  Muc tieu la backfill lich su

7.3. Ghi crawl run vao MongoDB
- Luu mot log giai doan `run` voi status `started`

7.4. Buoc sync danh sach ma - sync_listing()
- Goi `Listing(source='VND')`
- Lay toan bo danh sach ma
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo collection `raw_payloads`
  dataset = `listing`
- Loc giu lai cac ma co type la co phieu
- Chuan hoa ve cac cot:
  symbol, organ_name, exchange, company_type, industry, sector
- Upsert vao bang SQL `listing`
- Ghi crawl log vao Mongo collection `crawl_logs`

7.5. Resume crawl
- test.py goi `get_done_symbols()`
- Query `quote_history` de lay cac symbol da co ban ghi voi `trading_date = CURRENT_DATE`
- Cac ma da co du lieu ngay hom nay se bi bo qua
- Muc dich: neu job dang do hoac chay lai, khong crawl lai phan da xong

7.6. Chay theo batch
- BATCH_SIZE hien tai = 50
- Moi batch se lap qua tung symbol va chay 4 ham:
  sync_quotes()
  sync_company()
  sync_finance()
  sync_trading()

7.7. Sync quote - sync_quotes()
- Goi `Quote(symbol, source='VND').history(...)`
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo:
  dataset = `quote_history`
- Chuan hoa trading_date, open, high, low, close, volume
- Upsert vao SQL table `quote_history`
- Ghi crawl log `quotes`

7.8. Sync company - sync_company()
- Goi `Company(symbol, source='VCI').overview()`
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo:
  dataset = `company`
- Lay thong tin industry va sector
- Upsert vao SQL table `company`
- Ghi crawl log `company`

7.9. Sync finance - sync_finance()
- Goi `Finance(symbol, source='VCI').ratio()`
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo:
  dataset = `finance_ratio`
- Reset index, melt tu wide sang long format
- Chuan hoa ve:
  symbol, report_type, report_period, item_name, value
- Upsert vao SQL table `finance`
- Ghi crawl log `finance`

7.10. Sync trading - sync_trading()
- Goi `Trading(symbol, source='VCI')`
- Lay:
  foreign_trade()
  prop_trade()
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo:
  dataset = `trading_foreign`
  dataset = `trading_prop`
- Gop va upsert vao SQL table `trading`

- Goi them `Trading(symbol, source='CAFEF').order_stats()`
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo:
  dataset = `trading_order_stats`
- Upsert vao SQL table `trading_order_stats`

- Goi `summary()`
- Luu raw payload vao Mongo:
  dataset = `trading_summary`
- Upsert vao SQL table `trading_summary`

7.11. Ket thuc sync
- In progress tung batch
- Sau khi xong tat ca:
  ghi crawl log `run` voi status `completed`
- Goi `db.refresh_historical_aggregates(start=START_DATE)`
  de refresh continuous aggregates trong TimescaleDB


8. FLOW CHI TIET - TIMESCALEDB / SQL LAYER
------------------------------------------
timescale_utils.py la lop DB manager chinh.

8.1. Ket noi
- Ket noi vao DB `vnstock_ts`
- Tu dong `CREATE EXTENSION IF NOT EXISTS timescaledb CASCADE`

8.2. Tao bang metadata
- listing
- company
- finance

8.3. Tao bang time-series va hypertable
- quote_history
- trading
- trading_summary
- trading_order_stats

8.4. Toi uu TimescaleDB
- Compression policy
- Retention policy
- Continuous aggregate:
  quote_weekly
  quote_monthly

8.5. Muc dich cua SQL layer
- La source of truth cho phan tich du lieu
- Phuc vu query cho indicator, dashboard, export data va AI


9. FLOW CHI TIET - MONGODB LAYER
--------------------------------
mongo_utils.py la lop bo sung moi, khong thay the SQL.

9.1. Muc dich
- Luu raw API payload truoc khi transform
- Luu crawl logs theo run/stage/symbol
- Ho tro debug khi API doi schema, loi request, hoac can replay ETL

9.2. Collections hien tai
- raw_payloads
  Luu:
  run_id, dataset, symbol, source, payload, metadata, fetched_at

- crawl_logs
  Luu:
  run_id, stage, symbol, status, message, extra, logged_at

9.3. Tinh chat van hanh
- Neu MongoDB khong san sang hoac pymongo chua duoc cai:
  ETL van tiep tuc chay SQL nhu cu
- Nghia la MongoDB hien tai la lop optional, khong phai hard dependency


10. FLOW CHI TIET - COMPUTE INDICATORS
--------------------------------------
compute_indicators.py la nhanh xu ly trung gian giua ETL va AI.

10.1. Dau vao
- Doc du lieu tu `quote_history`

10.2. Xu ly
- Tinh cac chi bao trend:
  sma_10, sma_20, sma_50, ema_12, ema_26, macd, macd_signal, macd_hist, adx_14

- Tinh volatility:
  bb_upper, bb_mid, bb_lower, bb_pct_b, bb_width, atr_14

- Tinh volume:
  obv, vwap, cmf_20, volume_sma_20, volume_ratio

- Tinh candlestick patterns:
  doji, hammer, bullish engulfing, bearish engulfing, morning star, evening star

- Tinh target labels cho ML:
  direction_5d, direction_10d, return_5d

10.3. Dau ra
- Upsert vao bang `technical_indicators`

10.4. Che do chay
- Full:
  `python compute_indicators.py`

- Daily:
  `python compute_indicators.py --daily`

- Theo ma:
  `python compute_indicators.py --symbol TCB FPT VNM`

10.5. Diem quan trong
- Bang `technical_indicators` hien da duoc tich hop chay tu dong sau khi dong bo quote_history.
- Nho the ma khi ban chay cac script AI (thu cong) thi luon co du lieu chi bao moi nhat de du doan.


11. FLOW CHI TIET - EXPORT DATA CHO LSTM
----------------------------------------
11.1. export_data_lstm.py
- Join `quote_history` voi `technical_indicators`
- Loc theo 1 symbol
- Drop NaN
- Xuat ra `lstm_data.csv`
- Dung cho train/predict model rieng theo tung ma

11.2. export_all_lstm.py
- Join `quote_history` voi `technical_indicators`
- Lay toan bo thi truong
- Drop NaN
- Xuat ra `all_stocks_lstm_data.csv`
- Dung de train model tong cho nhieu ma


12. FLOW CHI TIET - AI SERVICE
------------------------------
ai_service.py la FastAPI service sinh du bao.

12.1. API
- GET /
  Health check

- POST /daily_predict_all
  Tao background task `run_predictions()`
  API tra ve ngay, khong doi model tinh xong

12.2. Trong run_predictions()
- Tao DatabaseManager
- Kiem tra ket noi DB
- Tim model:
  uu tien `global_stock_model.h5`
  neu khong co thi fallback `hpg_lstm_model.h5`

- Chon danh sach ma:
  neu model global: HPG, VNM, FPT, TCB, SSI
  neu model rieng: HPG

- Query data bang cach join:
  quote_history q
  technical_indicators t

- Features hien tai:
  close, volume, sma_10, sma_20, macd, bb_upper, bb_lower

- Neu moi symbol co it hon 60 dong:
  bo qua

- Neu du du lieu:
  scale data bang MinMaxScaler
  lay 60 phien gan nhat
  model.predict()
  inverse transform gia du doan
  tinh trend TANG/GIAM
  tim target_date la phien giao dich tiep theo
  ghi ket qua vao `ml_predictions`

12.3. Dau ra cuoi
- Bang `ml_predictions` luu:
  symbol, predict_date, target_date, predicted_close, trend, model_used

15. NHUNG LUONG CHUA TU DONG HOA HOAN TOAN
------------------------------------------
Hien tai van con cac thao tac mang tinh ky thuat nguoi dung tu chay:

- Chay pipeline AI/LSTM: gom export_data, train model, va predict.
- Cac tac vu nay phu thuoc phan ung thi truong va quy trinh kiem thu AI nen duoc giu doc lap (khong chay trong Airflow de toi uu he thong).


16. KET LUAN NGAN GON
--------------------
Flow hien tai cua du an la:

A. Tu dong hoa hang ngay (Airflow):
Airflow/Docker
-> test.py dong bo du lieu tu cac nguon VNStock
-> luu du lieu chuan hoa vao TimescaleDB
-> luu raw payload va crawl log vao MongoDB
-> refresh aggregate
-> compute_indicators.py cap nhat technical_indicators de san sang cho buoc ML.

B. Thao tac thu cong (khi can nghien cuu, chay AI):
-> export_data_lstm.py / export_all_lstm.py
-> train/predict LSTM (chay cac file AI tren may host cua ban)

Trang thai hien tai: he thong thu thap du lieu va tinh toan chi bao hoat dong on dinh, nhe nhang va tu dong hoan toan. Cac tac vu nang nhu chay AI/ML da duoc tach ra chay doc lap de tranh qua tai he thong.
