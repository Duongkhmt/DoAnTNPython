GIAI THICH DAY DU VE MO HINH DU BAO CHUNG KHOAN DANG TRAIN TRONG DU AN
=======================================================================

1. MO HINH NAY LA GI?
---------------------
Mo hinh AI trong du an nay khong phai la model chat, khong phai LLM, va cung khong phai model du doan "gia exact" cua co phieu.

Mo hinh thuc te dang duoc train va su dung trong du an la:

- Ten loai: LightGBM Ranker
- Objective: lambdarank
- Bai toan: cross-sectional ranking
- Muc tieu: xep hang co phieu nao co kha nang manh hon thi truong trong 5 phien toi

Noi ngan gon:
- Model khong tra loi cau hoi "mai gia VCB se la bao nhieu?"
- Model tra loi cau hoi "trong toan bo tap co phieu hom nay, ma nao co kha nang outperform VNINDEX trong 5 phien toi?"

Day la mot khac biet rat quan trong.

2. MUC DICH THAT SU CUA MODEL
-----------------------------
Model duoc xay dung de phuc vu mot quyet dinh dau tu thuc dung:

- Chon ra nhom co phieu manh nhat trong ngay
- Loai bo nhom co phieu yeu nhat trong ngay
- Tao diem xep hang de dashboard, API, hoac quy trinh giao dich co the dung duoc

Trong code, output cua model duoc dua ve cac nhom:

- TOP_STRONG: top 10 phan tram diem cao nhat
- TOP: tu 10 phan tram den 30 phan tram
- NEUTRAL: vung trung lap
- WEAK: tu 70 phan tram den duoi 90 phan tram
- WEAK_STRONG: 10 phan tram yeu nhat

Vi vay, trong du an nay, trong tam cua model khong phai "forecast gia tri tuyet doi", ma la:

- relative strength
- alpha so voi thi truong
- kha nang xep hang dung top-k co phieu

3. TAI SAO CHON KIEU MODEL NAY THAY VI REGRESSION HAY CLASSIFICATION?
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Trong chung khoan, du doan gia exact rat kho vi:

- nhieu nhieu
- noise cao
- bien dong phu thuoc tin tuc, dong tien, tam ly, vi mo
- ngay ca khi huong du doan dung, gia exact van co the sai xa

Neu dung regression:
- model phai doan mot con so lien tuc
- rat de bi sai so lon

Neu dung classification tang/giam:
- qua tho, vi chi cho biet len hay xuong
- khong giup xep uu tien co phieu nao tot hon co phieu nao

LightGBM Ranker phu hop hon vi:

- no hoc cach so sanh cac ma trong cung mot ngay
- no toi uu viec chon top-k
- no hop voi cach van hanh danh muc hon
- no gan voi quyet dinh thuc te cua nguoi dung: mua nhom manh nhat, tranh nhom yeu nhat

4. MODEL DUA TREN Y TUONG GI?
-----------------------------
Y tuong cot loi la:

"Mot co phieu tot khong nen duoc danh gia don doc, ma nen duoc dat trong boi canh toan thi truong cua cung ngay giao dich."

Vi du:
- RSI = 65 cua mot ma co the la tot trong ngay thi truong yeu
- nhung cung RSI = 65 co the khong dac biet neu ca thi truong deu dang rat manh

Vi vay model dung "cross-sectional ranking":
- trong tung trading_date, cac co phieu duoc dat len ban can voi nhau
- model hoc cach nhan dien ma nao xung dang dung tren nhung ma con lai

6. VI SAO KHONG HOC TRUC TIEP alpha_5d MA LAI TAO rank_label?
--------------------------------------------------------------
Model ranker khong hoc tot nhat tren gia tri thuc lien tuc theo kieu regression. No hoc tot hon khi du lieu duoc dua ve muc xep hang.

Trong notebook:

- alpha_5d duoc rank theo tung trading_date
- sau do chia thanh 5 muc nhan
- bien nhan do la rank_label

Cong thuc logic:
- tinh percentile cua alpha_5d trong ngay
- floor(percentile * 5)
- clip vao [0, 4]

Y nghia:
- nhom 4: manh nhat trong ngay
- nhom 0: yeu nhat trong ngay

Noi cach khac:
- model khong hoc "alpha = 3.2%"
- model hoc "ma nay thuoc nhom manh hon phan lon phan con lai trong ngay"

Dieu nay lam bai toan on dinh hon va sat muc tieu ranking hon.

19. LIGHTGBM RANKER TINH TOAN KIEU GI?
-------------------------------------
Day la phan can hieu that ro.

LightGBM la mot he gradient boosting tree.
No xay dung nhieu cay quyet dinh nho, roi cong don ket qua.

Trong bai toan nay:
- moi dong du lieu la mot co phieu tai mot trading_date
- cac dong duoc group theo trading_date
- trong tung group, model hoc cach dat score sao cho ma co alpha cao dung tren ma co alpha thap

Objective lambdarank khong toi uu MSE nhu regression.
No toi uu mot ham mat mat gan voi chat luong ranking, dac biet la NDCG.

Y tuong cua no:
- neu trong cung mot ngay, ma A dang ra phai xep tren ma B
- nhung model dang xep nguoc
- gradient se day score cua A len va/hoac keo score cua B xuong

Qua nhieu cay:
- model hoc duoc nhung nguong tach feature
- vi du: neu rel_momentum_20 cao, volume_ratio tot, vnindex_rsi vua phai, stoch_k tot hon phan lon thi diem tang

21. CAC HYPERPARAMETER CHINH CO Y NGHIA GI?
------------------------------------------
learning_rate = 0.03
- moi cay chi dieu chinh nhe
- hoc cham hon nhung an toan hon

n_estimators = 1500
- toi da 1500 cay/iterations neu can
- ket hop voi early stopping

num_leaves = 63
- moi cay co toi da 63 la
- cang lon cang hoc quan he phuc tap hon

min_child_samples = 40
- moi nut can du mau toi thieu
- giam overfit

subsample = 0.8
- moi lan chi lay 80 phan tram mau
- tang kha nang tong quat hoa

colsample_bytree = 0.8
- moi cay chi lay 80 phan tram feature
- giam phu thuoc qua muc vao mot so feature

reg_alpha = 0.2, reg_lambda = 1.0
- regularization L1 va L2
- giam do phuc tap huu hieu cua model

metric = ndcg
- do chat luong ranking
- phu hop hon accuracy hay RMSE

eval_at = [10, 20, 50]
- danh gia kha nang xep hang dung o top 10, top 20, top 50
- rat sat voi muc tieu chon danh muc top-k

22. NDCG LA GI? TAI SAO NO QUAN TRONG?
--------------------------------------
NDCG = Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain.

No la metric danh cho ranking.

Tu tuong:
- item dung xuat hien cang cao trong bang xep hang thi cang co gia tri
- sai o top dau bi phat nang hon sai o cuoi bang

Trong dau tu, dieu nay rat hop ly vi:
- nguoi dung thuong chi mua mot so ma top dau
- neu top 10 chon dung, model co gia tri thuc te cao
- duoi bang xep hang co sai mot chut cung it anh huong hon

25. NGUOI DUNG NEN NHIN VAO GI DE BIET MODEL CO ON KHONG?
---------------------------------------------------------
Day la phan rat quan trong neu muon "theo" model mot cach nghiem tuc.

Can nhin toi thieu 8 nhom tieu chi:

1. Top-k alpha co duong khong?
- Neu top 10 / top 20 co avg_alpha duong mot cach on dinh, day la dau hieu tot

2. Alpha win rate co > 50% khong?
- Neu so ma outperform thi truong chi quanh 50% hoac thap hon, mo hinh yeu

3. Top-k return co cao hon universe khong?
- Neu khong hon trung binh thi truong, model ranking co the chua co gia tri

4. Validation va test co giong nhau khong?
- Neu valid dep nhung test xau, kha nang cao la overfit

5. Hieu qua co on dinh theo ngay khong?
- Khong chi nhin mean
- Can xem do lech, drawdown, chuoi ngay thua

6. Feature importance co hop ly khong?
- Neu top feature la cac bien vo nghia, can canh giac
- Neu top feature nam trong nhom momentum, relative strength, volume, regime thi hop ly hon

7. Ti le skipped_symbols co cao khong?
- Trong pipeline du doan hang ngay, neu qua nhieu ma bi bo qua vi thieu feature, chat luong van hanh giam

8. Model co con phu hop voi che do thi truong hien tai khong?
- Thi truong thay doi che do
- model tot trong trend market co the yeu trong sideway hoac bear market

32. UU DIEM CUA MODEL NAY
-------------------------
1. Dung bai toan hon regression gia exact
- Sat hon voi cach ra quyet dinh dau tu

2. Chiu duoc quan he phi tuyen
- Tree-based model bat duoc nguong va tuong tac feature

3. Khong can scale feature phuc tap nhu nhieu model khac
- LightGBM xu ly kha tot voi feature da dang

4. Co the giai thich duoc mot phan qua feature importance
- Tot hon mot so mo hinh qua den

5. Suy luan nhanh, de deploy
- Rat hop voi daily batch prediction

6. Tap trung vao alpha
- Tot cho bai toan stock selection

33. NHUOC DIEM VA GIOI HAN CUA MODEL
------------------------------------
1. Khong nhin thay tin tuc, vi mo, ket qua kinh doanh
- Neu bien dong den tu thong tin phi ky thuat, model co the cham phan ung

2. De bi regime shift
- Quy luat hoc tu qua khu co the giam tac dung khi thi truong doi pha

3. Co nguy co overfit neu tuning qua da
- Nhat la trong bai toan tai chinh co signal yeu

4. Feature phu thuoc manh vao quality cua bang technical_indicators
- Tinh sai feature la hu model

5. Chi danh gia suc manh tuong doi 5 phien
- Khong phai model dau tu dai han

6. Score khong phai probability
- Neu user hieu sai ai_score se rat nguy hiem

34. CAC RUI RO PHUONG PHAP LUAN CAN CANH GIAC
---------------------------------------------
Neu muon danh gia model mot cach chat che, phai canh giac:

- Look-ahead bias
- Survivorship bias
- Data leakage
- Overfitting tren validation
- Chon metric dep nhung khong co gia tri giao dich
- Khong tinh chi phi giao dich, truot gia, thanh khoan thuc

Vi du:
- top-k co avg_alpha dep tren file test
- nhung neu trade that ma thanh khoan kem, spread cao, thi loi nhuan that co the xau

35. NGUOI DUNG NEN TIN MODEL DEN MUC NAO?
-----------------------------------------
Nen xem model nhu:
- bo loc co hoi
- cong cu uu tien ma
- mot lop ho tro ra quyet dinh

Khong nen xem model nhu:
- may tien tri chac chan
- he thong thay the hoan toan phan tich con nguoi
- cong cu ra lenh khong can kiem soat rui ro

Cach dung dung:
- dung model de loc top ung vien
- ket hop voi quan tri von, tin tuc, su kien, va thanh khoan
- theo doi hieu qua theo rolling window

36. MUON BIET MODEL "OKE DE THEO" THI CAN KIEM TRA GI DINH KY?
--------------------------------------------------------------
Nen lap dashboard hoac bao cao dinh ky cho cac chi so sau:

- Top 10 avg_alpha theo ngay
- Top 20 avg_alpha theo ngay
- Win rate alpha > 0
- Win rate return > 0
- So sanh top-k voi universe
- Phan bo signal TOP/TOP_STRONG/WEAK
- So ma bi skipped vi thieu feature
- Hieu qua theo industry/sector
- Hieu qua theo tung regime thi truong
- Feature drift va distribution drift

Neu cac chi so nay suy giam trong mot thoi gian dai:
- can retrain
- can xem lai feature
- can xem thi truong da doi che do chua

37. RETRAIN NEN LAM NHU THE NAO?
--------------------------------
Ve ly thuyet va thuc te, nen:

- cap nhat them du lieu moi
- giu nguyen logic split theo thoi gian
- danh gia out-of-sample
- so sanh model moi voi model cu tren cung bo test/forward period
- khong thay model chi vi metric dep hon rat nho

Nen co cac ban model version nhu:
- lgbm_alpha_5d_ranker_v1
- v2
- v3

Va ghi ro:
- train den ngay nao
- feature set nao
- metric top-k ra sao

38. VI SAO FILE JSON FEATURE RAT QUAN TRONG?
-------------------------------------------
File lgbm_alpha_5d_features.json luu:

- model_type
- target
- danh sach feature
- min_group_size
- n_label_bins

No la hop dong giua train va inference.

Neu train mot model voi 49 feature ma khi predict lai dua 48 feature hoac thu tu khac:
- ket qua se sai
- thậm chi crash

Vi vay file nay la phan rat quan trong cua deploy.

39. KHI XEM MOT MODEL TREE-BASED NHU THE NAY, CAN DAT NHUNG CAU HOI NAO?
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Target co dung voi bai toan kinh doanh khong?
2. Split theo thoi gian co sach khong?
3. Feature co duoc tao ma khong lo data leakage khong?
4. Metric co phu hop voi quyet dinh top-k khong?
5. Test co that su out-of-sample khong?
6. Hieu qua co on dinh hay chi dep o mot vai giai doan?
7. Co tinh chi phi giao dich va thanh khoan chua?
8. Quy trinh retrain, version, monitoring da co chua?

Neu tra loi duoc nhung cau hoi nay, moi co co so noi model "oke de theo".

40. KET LUAN NGAN GON NHUNG CHINH XAC
-------------------------------------
Mo hinh trong du an nay la mot he thong chon co phieu dua tren xep hang, su dung LightGBM Ranker de tim cac ma co alpha 5 ngay cao hon so voi VNINDEX.

No dua tren:
- du lieu gia va khoi luong lich su
- cac chi bao ky thuat
- feature dong tien
- candlestick pattern
- boi canh VNINDEX
- feature xep hang tuong doi trong ngay

No tinh toan bang:
- tao feature
- tao alpha_5d
- bien alpha thanh rank_label
- group theo trading_date
- train lambdarank de toi uu chat luong xep hang
- sinh ai_score va ai_signal cho tung ma moi ngay

Trong tam cua no la:
- relative strength
- top-k stock selection
- outperform thi truong trong 5 phien toi

Muon danh gia no co tot hay khong, khong duoc chi nhin mot vai du doan dung sai.
Phai nhin he thong:

- top-k alpha
- win rate
- out-of-sample test
- do on dinh qua thoi gian
- feature importance
- drift
- va kha nang song duoc trong dieu kien giao dich thuc te

Neu can noi mot cach thang:
- day la mot model ranking co dinh huong dung cho stock selection ngan han
- no hop ly ve mat thiet ke
- nhung gia tri that cua no phai duoc chung minh bang backtest sach, test out-of-sample, va theo doi live performance lien tuc
