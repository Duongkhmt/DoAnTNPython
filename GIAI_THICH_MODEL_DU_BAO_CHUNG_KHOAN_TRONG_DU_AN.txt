4. TARGET alpha_rank_pct LA GI?
-------------------------------
Day la khái niệm quan trong nhat cua model moi.

Truoc het:
- alpha_5d = return_5d_stock - return_5d_vnindex

Trong do:
- return_5d_stock: muc tang/giam cua co phieu sau 5 phien
- return_5d_vnindex: muc tang/giam cua VNINDEX sau 5 phien
- alpha_5d: muc vuot troi cua co phieu so voi thi truong

Sau do model khong hoc truc tiep alpha_5d raw.
No tao:

- alpha_rank_pct = rank percentile cua alpha_5d trong tung trading_date

Nghia la:
- moi ngay, tat ca co phieu duoc sap hang theo alpha_5d
- ma nao manh nhat se co alpha_rank_pct gan 1
- ma nao yeu nhat se co alpha_rank_pct gan 0

Y nghia ly thuyet:
- model hoc "vi tri tuong doi" cua co phieu trong ngay
- khong hoc gia tri alpha tuyet doi

Day la mot y tuong hop ly cho stock selection vi:
- giao dich thuong can biet ma nao tot hon ma nao

12. TRAIN / TEST / VALIDATION BAY GIO CHIA NHU THE NAO?
------------------------------------------------------
Ban moi su dung 2 lop danh gia:

1. Walk-forward validation 5 folds
2. Production train/test cuoi cung theo TEST_CUTOFF

Walk-forward validation:
- 5 folds
- moi fold dung 360 ngay train
- 40 ngay test
- cuon chieu theo thoi gian

Logic:
- fold sau luon test o giai doan tuong lai hon fold truoc
- day la cach kiem tra model co on dinh qua nhieu giai doan thi truong hay khong

Production split:
- TEST_CUTOFF = 2025-10-01
- train_full: trading_date < 2025-10-01
- test_full: trading_date >= 2025-10-01

Day la cach chia dung cho bai toan tai chinh vi:
- tranh look-ahead bias
- mo phong thuc te "hoc qua khu, test tuong lai"

13. WALK-FORWARD VALIDATION CO Y NGHIA GI?
-----------------------------------------
Walk-forward validation rat quan trong trong chung khoan.

Neu chi chia mot lan train/test:
- ket qua co the phu thuoc vao mot giai doan thi truong cu the

Neu dung walk-forward:
- model duoc kiem tra tren nhieu cua so thoi gian lien tiep
- giup xem signal co on dinh khong
- giam nguy co an may do mot doan test dep

No tra loi cau hoi:

"Model co thuc su co ich o nhieu che do thi truong khac nhau khong?"

15. RANK IC LA GI?
-----------------
Rank IC = Spearman correlation giua:
- score model du doan
va
- alpha_5d thuc te

No do:
- model co sap dung thu tu manh/yeu cua co phieu trong ngay hay khong

Neu Rank IC duong:
- score cao thuong di cung alpha thuc te cao

Neu Rank IC am:
- model xep hang nguoc

Neu Rank IC gan 0:
- model gan nhu khong co suc phan biet

Trong tai chinh, Rank IC la mot metric rat hop ly cho stock selection vi:
- bai toan cot loi la sap thu tu
- khong phai du doan dung con so tuyet doi

No khong toi uu ranking truc tiep bang lambdarank nua, ma hoc ra mot score lien tuc roi dung score do de xep hang co phieu trong tung ngay.

Phien ban moi dat trong tam vao:

- xu huong gia ngan han
- suc manh tuong doi voi thi truong
- dong tien
- bien dong
- va co them mot lop market regime filter de phong thu khi VNINDEX xau

No duoc danh gia bang cach chat che hon so voi ban cu:

- walk-forward validation 5 folds
- Rank IC
- IR
- top-k alpha
- top-k return

Neu phai noi mot cach ngan gon:

"Day la mot he thong stock ranking ngan han theo huong toi gian va de tong quat hoa hon, dung regressor de du doan percentile alpha tuong lai, sau do xep hang de chon nhom co phieu manh nhat va tranh nhom yeu nhat."
